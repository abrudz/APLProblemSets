Creating the matrix

The "outer product" operator applies its function operand to all combinations of elements of its left and right argument arrays. 

      f←{⍺+⍵}
      1 2 3 ∘.f 10 20 30
11 21 31
12 22 32
13 23 33

Reduce-first ⌿ on a matrix will reduce along columns instead of rows. 

      ∘.÷⍨⍳3
1 0.5 0.3333333333
2 1   0.6666666667
3 1.5 1    
       
      +/∘.÷⍨⍳3               ⍝ Sum of rows
1.833333333 3.666666667 5.5

      +⌿∘.÷⍨⍳3               ⍝ Sum of columns
6 3 2
