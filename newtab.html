5: Scan and windowed reduction

The "scan" operator \ works similarly to reduce /, but it gives intermediate values. 

      ×\⍳10
1 2 6 24 120 720 5040 40320 362880 3628800

If you give a reduction a number n to its left, it will do a windowed sum of size n on its argument. 

      2×/⍳10   ⍝ Pair-wise products
2 6 12 20 30 42 56 72 90
